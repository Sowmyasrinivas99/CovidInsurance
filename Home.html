What is Coronavirus Health Insurance?

              A coronavirus health insurance is a customized health insurance policy dedicated to covering hospitalization
and treatment costs of the covid-19. There are various types of covid-19 health insurances online to choose from in India;
such as the corona rakshak cover, corona kavach policy or better, you may even just get a comprehensive health insurance 
that also covers the coronavirus among other illnesses, diseases and healthcare benefits.
